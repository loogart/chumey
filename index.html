Our strategyPatience for profit.

1. Find

Search for the right property. A good return on investment cannot be accomplished without finding the right property.

2. Acquire

Buy the property. Paying the right price for the property can be the make or break step for the investment.

3. Stabilize

Fix up, market and rent property. This is essential to to achieve appropriate rent and/or resale value.

4. Manage

Be the primary interface with the property management company managing tenants, repairs and improvement services.

5. Sell

Sell the property. Maximizing the exit of the investment and reaping the rewards.
